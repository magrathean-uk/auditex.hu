Built for careful operators.

Auditex keeps setup guidance, live-run checks, and customer handoff material close to the evidence. It is audit-only for public v1 work: no remediation automation, no hosted tenant data store, no App Store wrapper.

  • Before accessGenerate Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace setup plans and know the required read-only scopes.
  • During runsUse safe throttling, probe-first mode, explicit collector presets, and saved auth contexts when appropriate.
  • After runsVerify the report pack, compare runs, export selected artifacts, and hand off only reviewed material.
