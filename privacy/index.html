Auditex
Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Auditex keeps tenant evidence local by default. The product runs in the operator environment and does not host, relay, or store customer tenant evidence on Magrathean infrastructure unless an operator deliberately sends material to us for support or under a separate written agreement.

Data Controller and Roles

MAGRATHEAN UK LTD., a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 16955343) with registered office at 16 Caledonian Court West Street, Watford, England, WD17 1RY, is the controller for personal data we process for our website, support, licensing, security, and customer communications. For tenant evidence collected by Auditex in your environment, the tenant owner, customer, or authorised operator is normally the controller, and Magrathean is not a processor unless a separate written processing agreement says so.

What Auditex Reads

Auditex is designed for authorised Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace tenant audit work. It may read configuration, posture, permission, identity, group, device, sharing, domain, audit-log, API-call, and security-control metadata needed to produce evidence, blockers, reports, and proof tables. This data may include personal data such as names, email addresses, group membership, role assignments, device metadata, OAuth grant metadata, sharing metadata, and audit event metadata.

No Content-Read Policy

Auditex audit collectors must not read Gmail message bodies, Google Drive file content, Exchange mailbox body content, SharePoint file content, or OneDrive file content. Some provider scopes have broad or write-capable names even when used for read-only posture checks; Auditex records scope risk and observed API usage so the operator can review what was attempted.

Local Processing and Storage

Raw evidence, normalized records, API inventory, data-handling declarations, blockers, validation reports, report packs, customer handoff packs, checksums, and MCP context are written to the local output directory selected by the operator. Auditex does not upload these bundles to MAGRATHEAN UK LTD. servers by default.

Credentials and Authentication Material

Azure CLI sessions, OAuth tokens, refresh material, Google service-account files, OAuth client files, OAuth cache files, app credentials, bearer material, and signing keys remain in the local operator environment or platform credential stores configured by the operator. Do not send those materials to us, commit them to source control, or place them in customer packs.

Website, Support, and Access Requests

If you email us, request access, report a security issue, or send support material, we process the contact details and content you provide so we can respond, administer the relationship, protect the product, and keep records of the request. Do not send tenant evidence, secrets, or regulated data unless we have agreed a secure support route in writing.

Legal Bases

For contact, licensing, security, and support data that we process as controller, we rely on contract where processing is necessary to provide requested services, legitimate interests where we operate, secure, improve, and defend the product, consent where required for optional communications, and legal obligation where we must keep records or respond to lawful requests. Operators remain responsible for their own lawful basis for tenant-audit processing.

Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal data and do not use Auditex tenant evidence for advertising. We may use service providers for email, hosting, security, legal, accounting, and operational support. Where a service provider processes personal data for us, we use appropriate contractual controls. Tenant evidence is shared with us only if you deliberately send it.

International Transfers

We primarily operate from the United Kingdom. If support, email, hosting, or security providers process personal data outside the UK or EEA, we use appropriate safeguards such as adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses where required by data-protection law.

Retention

Auditex output retention is controlled by the operator because the output is local. Delete output directories, auth caches, exported reports, and customer packs when they are no longer needed for the authorised audit purpose. We keep contact, support, security, and business records only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, legal compliance, dispute handling, and auditability.

Security

Auditex is designed around local evidence handling, no external analytics, read-only audit contracts, API inventory, customer-pack verification, and no-content-read validation. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Operators must protect the machines, credentials, output folders, transfer channels, and recipients used for each run.

Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability where applicable, and withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise rights against data we control, contact contact@magrathean.uk. For tenant evidence controlled by your organisation or customer, direct requests to that controller.

Children

Auditex is an enterprise and operator tool. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through the product.

Complaints

You may complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint. We would appreciate the opportunity to address concerns directly first.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Last updated date shows when the current version took effect. Material changes will be published on this page or notified through an appropriate product or customer channel.
